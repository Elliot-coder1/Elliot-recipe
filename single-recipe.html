Chin chin

Chin chin recipe is on of the easiest recipes on our list, all you need is (flour, sugar, little salt, milk, baking powder, milk flavor, and some water.)

prep time

15mins.

cook time

5mins.

Servings

6 servings

Tags : Chicken Breakfast Chin chin Food

Instructions

step 1

In a blow add all your ingredients.

step 2

Next add little water and mix to harden,after that with your rolling stick roll to flaten.

step 3

with a knife cut into small pieaces any shape or size of your choice.

step 4

In a frying pan put oli and wait to hot, then put some in the pan medium heat until it turns light brown as shown on screen. After that your chin is ready to be serve.

ingredients

1. 1 and 1/2 cups of flour.

2. 1 cup of sugar and table spoon of salt.

3. milk and milk flavor (use as desire).

4. half table spoon of baking powder, and water

tools

wisker.

rolling stick.

Measuring cups and spoon.
