Spaghetti

Our lovely Spaghetti with hot dogs is a wonder and have to be made with love and care

prep time

15mins.

cook time

20mins.

Servings

4 servings

Tags : Chicken Breakfast Chin chin Food

Instructions

step 1

In a pot of boiling water add your spaghetti to boil .

step 2

cut your hot dogs into small size and set aside.

step 3

wash your par-boil spaghetti and set aside .

step 4

In a frying pan add little oil once hot add tomatoe paste, allow to get cooked then add your spices(salt, grinded pepper maggi and little curry), then add your spaghetti and chopped hot dogs add little water and allow to cook untill dry. Now your food is ready to be served .

ingredients

1. 2 packs of spaghetti

2. hot dogs

3. spices (salt, grinded pepper maggi and little curry).

4. tin tomatoes, oil and some water.

tools

pots.

frying pan.
