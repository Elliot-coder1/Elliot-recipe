Corn dogs

Corn this easiest of all let's go

prep time

10mins.

cook time

15mins.

Servings

6 servings

Tags : Chicken Breakfast Chin chin Food

Instructions

step 1

in a deep small blow add flour, liquid milk, eggs, sugar salt and water for it to become a light paste.

step 2

cut your hot dog into two equall halves.

step 3

put the hot dogs in the stick and into the paste leaving only the stick part visible.

step 4

In a frying pan put oli and wait to heat then put the corn dogs in the oil to fry untill golden brown and now your corn dogs are ready to be serve.

ingredients

1. hot dogs.

2. flour.

3. liquid milk.

4. eggs, sugar, salt

tools

wisker.

corns dogs stick .
