Jollof rice and chicken

To make Jollof rice certain steps must be taken in other to make it perfect.

prep time

3omins.

cook time

1hrs.

Servings

4 servings

Tags : Chicken Breakfast Chin chin Food

Instructions

step 1

wash your rice with clean water and par-boil to prepare for second washing.

step 2

Next in a blow wash your tomatoes, fresh pepper and onions with some gralic and place in the oven to have a brunt top 2mins at most (if your desire), then blend in a blender not too smooth once that's done set aside.

step 3

now time for our chicken which we will wash and set aside. Then in a blow add oil, some grinded fresh pepper, onions and gralic with ginger, chicken maggi and salt and mix, next rub on the chicken and let it rest for 5mins, 10 at most. After that place in an airfryer and allow to work its magic .

step 4

now our par-boil rice wash clean 4 times and set aside now we fry our mix (tomatoes,pepper,onions and gralic) with tin tomatoes for 5mins then add on spices(curry, thyme maggi salt bay leaves) to taste.

step 5

now we put a clean pot on fire add then fried mix and our rice and water to nearly fill the pot, then use thin foil fill cover the pot (to prevent heat from leaving the pot) and cover with pot cover to cook untill dry after that your food is ready to be served.

ingredients

1. half basket of rice.

2. tin tomatoes, fresh tomatoes,pepper, onions, and gralic.

3. Chicken.

4. And our spices(curry, thyme maggi salt bay leaves,chicken maggi ginger and oil)

tools

blender.

air fryer.

pots.
