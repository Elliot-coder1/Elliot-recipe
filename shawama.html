Shawama

chicken and beef shawama is great but have you tried them both? come on lets make some

prep time

30mins.

cook time

5/10mins.

Servings

6 servings

Tags : Chicken Breakfast Chin chin Food

Instructions

step 1

slice your cabagge and carrot then set aside .

step 2

Next in a pot add your chicken breast season it and allow to cook, then in another pot add your meat season and and also cook.

step 3

Once they are ready cut into small pieces, then in a blow add your sliced vegetables some kechup, Meyo(bama) grinded pepper, salt,and our meat and chicken then mix till there is nothing to mix and then set aside .

step 4

then lay our shawama bread and then add our mix and two hot dogs and wrap repeat this process depending on how many you are making then put in a pan with low heat place our shawama to worm now our shawama is ready.

ingredients

1. shawama bread

2. Cabagge and carrot.

3. Chicken and meat.

4. Moyo(bama), kechup,grinded pepper and salt

5. Hot dogs

tools

Pots .

Pans.
